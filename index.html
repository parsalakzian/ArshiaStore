Scary Minecraft map

Story Mode!

In this Minecraft world, characters, storyline and attractive battles
are used, which makes the gameplay very exciting!

Multiplayer

In this Minecraft world you can easily play with your friends with up to 4
people and enjoy this Minecraft world! Note that if there are more than 4 of you, they can destroy the world!

The OutLaw & Victims

Outlaw hunts down victims and won't stop until he kills them! You can hide in the closets!

Puzzles & Fights

The puzzles are easy to solve, but it is better to play with your friends for a better experience of fights and better puzzle solving!

Locations & Save Rooms

It is better to save your game in the save room after completing each stage and solving the puzzle so that your efforts are not lost!
